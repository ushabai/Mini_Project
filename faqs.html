Frequently Asked Questions

Account Related

How do I create an account?

Click the Sign Up button on the homepage. Enter your name, email address, and password, then submit the registration form. A verification email will be sent to activate your account.

I forgot my password. What should I do?
  • Click "Forgot Password" on login page.
  • Enter email and follow instructions.
How can I change my email address?

Visit Account Settings and choose "Change Email Address". Enter your new email and verify it using the confirmation link.

Payments

Which payment methods are accepted?

We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, and selected online banking options.

How do I download invoices?

Open Billing History from your dashboard and click the Download Invoice button next to the desired transaction.

Membership

How do I upgrade my plan?

Open Membership Settings and select "Upgrade Plan". Choose the desired plan and complete the payment process to activate your upgraded membership instantly.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Navigate to Membership Settings and select "Manage Subscription". Click Cancel Subscription and confirm your choice.
