Popular Questions

How do I reset my password?

Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page.Enter your registered email address and follow the instructions sent to your inbox.

Hpw to update profile?

Go to your account settings and select "Edit Profile". Update the required information and click Save Changes.

How to cancel subscription?

Navigate to Membership Settings and select "Manage Subscription". Click Cancel Subscription and confirm your choice.

How to change email?

Visit Account Settings and choose "Change Email Address". Enter your new email and verify it using the confirmation link.
